# Personal Skills

Personal productivity, growth, and life management capabilities.

## What Goes Here

Skills for personal development, productivity, health, and life management:

- Learning strategies and skill acquisition
- Time management and productivity systems
- Health and wellness tracking
- Personal finance management
- Habit formation and tracking
- Note-taking and knowledge management
- Goal setting and achievement
- Personal project management

## Examples

- `learning-strategy.md` - How you learn new skills and concepts
- `time-management.md` - Your time management approach
- `note-taking.md` - Note-taking and PKM (Personal Knowledge Management) system
- `habit-tracking.md` - Habit formation and maintenance
- `health-tracking.md` - Health and fitness approaches
- `finance-management.md` - Personal finance tracking
- `goal-setting.md` - Goal setting and achievement framework
- `reading.md` - Reading and retention strategies
- `meditation.md` - Meditation or mindfulness practices
- `exercise.md` - Fitness routines and approaches

## When to Use Personal Skills

Load when working on personal development, life management, or productivity tasks.

**Example:** Learning strategy + Note-taking + Goal setting = Creating a learning plan for a new skill

## Why Personal Skills Matter

Personal skills help AI assist you with:
- Learning new technologies efficiently
- Managing your time and priorities
- Tracking personal goals and habits
- Maintaining work-life balance
- Personal projects and side hustles

Even though these are "personal," they directly impact your professional effectiveness.
