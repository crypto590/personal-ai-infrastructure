# Business Skills

Business-focused capabilities for strategy, analysis, and stakeholder management.

## What Goes Here

Skills related to business operations, strategy, and non-technical problem solving:

- Business analysis and requirements gathering
- Stakeholder communication and management
- Project planning and execution
- Product strategy and management
- Data analysis and reporting
- Financial analysis and modeling
- Marketing and growth strategies
- Process optimization
- Strategic planning

## Examples

- `business-analysis.md` - Requirements gathering and analysis
- `stakeholder-management.md` - Working with stakeholders
- `project-planning.md` - Project planning and tracking
- `product-strategy.md` - Product thinking and strategy
- `data-analysis.md` - Business data analysis
- `financial-modeling.md` - Financial analysis and modeling
- `process-optimization.md` - Business process improvement
- `roadmap-planning.md` - Strategic roadmap development
- `user-research.md` - User research methodologies
- `presentation.md` - Presentation and pitch strategies

## When to Use Business Skills

Load when working on business planning, strategy, stakeholder communication, or non-technical problem solving.

**Example:** Business analysis + Stakeholder management + Roadmap planning = Gathering requirements and building a product roadmap
